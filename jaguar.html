Information

  • Scientific Name Panthera onca
  • Place Origin Central and South America
  • History Sacred in Maya and Aztec cultures; symbol of power and the night.
  • Size 45–120 kg; shoulder height 63–76 cm
  • Life Span 11-15 years in the wild; up to 20 years in captivity
  • Characteristics Strong, stocky build, golden coat with rosettes containing central spots.
  • Temperament Bold, powerful, enjoys water, and has an extremely strong bite.

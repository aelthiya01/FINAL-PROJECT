Information

  • Place Origin Ancient Persia (Iran)
  • History Brought to Europe in the 1600s; the blue color became one of the most popular and elegant Persian varieties.
  • Size Medium–Large
  • Life Span 16-20 years
  • Characteristics Long dense coat, flat face, blue-grey fur that appears silvery.
  • Temperament Calm, gentle, loves quiet households; prefers lounging over active play.
  • Care tips High maintenance; requires daily brushing to prevent mats and tangles.

Fun Fact!

Did you know? Their fur is so dense that it requires daily brushing to prevent mats.

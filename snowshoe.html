Information

  • Place Origin USA
  • History Developed from Siamese × American Shorthair, creating white paws and blue eyes.
  • Size Medium
  • Life Span 9-15 years
  • Characteristics Blue eyes, pointed pattern, four white “snowshoe” feet.
  • Temperament Vocal, friendly, social, and playful.
  • Care tips Attention and companionship, daily play, nail trimming.

Fun Fact!

Did you know? Their unique markings vary so much that no two Snowshoes look alike.

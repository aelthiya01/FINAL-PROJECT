Information

  • Place Origin USA (descended from Abyssinians)
  • History Longhair gene in Abyssinians was initially considered an oddity before becoming a recognized breed.
  • Size Medium
  • Life Span 12-16 years
  • Characteristics Long plumed tail, ticked coat, athletic body.
  • Temperament Very active, intelligent, loves climbing, and forms strong bonds with owners.
  • Care tips Climbing trees, daily play, brushing to prevent tangles.

Fun Fact!

Did you know? Called the “Fox Cat” due to their fluffy tail and reddish tones.

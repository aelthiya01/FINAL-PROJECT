Information

  • Scientific Name Panthera pardus
  • Place Origin Africa and Asia
  • History Exceptionally adaptable cat found in savannas, mountains, and forests. Featured heavily in African folklore.
  • Size 17–90 kg; shoulder height 60–70 cm
  • Life Span 10 to 12 years in the wild. Up to 23 years in captivity
  • Characteristics Rosette-patterned coat, powerful jaws, and superb climbing abilities.
  • Temperament Stealthy, solitary, elusive, and extremely adaptable to different environments.

Fun Fact!

Did you know? Leopards often drag prey up trees to keep it safe from lions and hyenas.

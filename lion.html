LION
(PANTHERA LEO)
PLACE ORIGIN:
Sub-Saharan Africa; Small Asiatic Population in India
HISTORY:
Once widespread across Eurasia; revered in many ancient cultures. Lions are the only truly social big cats, forming prides that work together for hunting and protection.
SIZE

150-250 KG;
1.1-1.2 M SHOULDER HEIGHT

CHARACTERISTICS

Muscular build, golden coat, tufted tail, and for males, a distinctive mane that signals dominance and maturity.

TEMPERAMENT:
Cooperative, territorial, and protective of their pride. Females handle most of the hunting.
FUN FACT:
A lion's roar can be heard up to 8 km away, used to mark territory and communicate.

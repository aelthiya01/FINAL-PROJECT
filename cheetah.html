Information

  • Scientific Name Acinonyx jubatus
  • Place Origin Africa; very small population in Iran
  • History Domesticated by ancient Egyptian and Indian aristocracy as hunting companions.
  • Size 20–72 kg; shoulder height 70–90 cm
  • Life Span 8-10 years in the wild; 12-15 years in captivity
  • Characteristics Slim lightweight body, long legs, deep chest, and black “tear marks.”
  • Temperament Non-aggressive and timid compared to other big cats; rely on speed rather than strength.

Fun Fact!

Did you know? They can accelerate from 0–100 km/h in just 3 seconds, faster than most sports cars.

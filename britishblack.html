Information

  • Place Origin UK
  • History British Shorthairs with a solid black coat were bred for uniform color and plush texture.
  • Size Medium–Large
  • Life Span 12-20 years
  • Characteristics Velvet-like black coat, round build, large orange eyes.
  • Temperament Easygoing, loyal, and gentle.
  • Care tips Brush weekly, maintain healthy weight, provide scratching posts.

Fun Fact!

Did you know? Their coats shine with a deep bronze tint under sunlight.

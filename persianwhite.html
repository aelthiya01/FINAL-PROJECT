Information

  • Place Origin Iran
  • History Became famous in Victorian England where white-coated Persians were considered prestigious.
  • Size Medium–Large
  • Life Span 15-20 years
  • Characteristics Pure white fur, blue or copper eyes, round face.
  • Temperament Affectionate, calm, and quiet; prefers predictable routines.
  • Care tips Frequent grooming, tear-stain cleaning, indoor environment.

Fun Fact!

Did you know? Blue-eyed white Persians may have a higher chance of deafness due to a genetic trait.

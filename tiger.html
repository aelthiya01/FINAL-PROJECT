Information

  • Scientific Name Panthera tigris
  • Place Origin Asia
  • History Largest cat species, symbolizing strength in Asian cultures. Tigers once ranged widely but now survive in fragmented populations.
  • Size 90–300 kg; shoulder height 90–110 cm
  • Life Span 10-15 years in the wild; up to 16 years in captivity
  • Characteristics Orange coat with black stripes, powerful limbs, and excellent night vision.
  • Temperament Solitary, territorial, cautious, but incredibly powerful hunters. They enjoy water and are strong swimmers.

Fun Fact!

Did you know? No two tigers have the same stripe pattern—each is as unique as a fingerprint.

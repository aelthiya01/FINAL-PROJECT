Information

  • Place Origin Thailand (Siam)
  • History Sacred cats of ancient temples; prized by royalty.
  • Size Medium
  • Life Span 15-20 years
  • Characteristics Slender body, blue almond-shaped eyes, short coat with color points.
  • Temperament Extremely vocal, affectionate, clever, and social.
  • Care tips Lots of mental stimulation, companionship, warm beds.

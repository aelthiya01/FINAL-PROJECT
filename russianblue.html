Information

  • Place Origin Russia (Archangel Islands)
  • History Sailors brought them to Europe in the 1800s; loved for their elegant blue coats.
  • Size Medium
  • Life Span 12-15 years
  • Characteristics Silver-tipped fur, vivid green eyes, graceful build.
  • Temperament Reserved with strangers but deeply affectionate to family; very intelligent.
  • Care tips Soft brushing weekly, puzzle toys, climbing areas.

Fun Fact!

Did you know? They produce less Fel d1 protein, making them more tolerable to some people with allergies.

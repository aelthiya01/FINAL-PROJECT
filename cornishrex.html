Information

  • Place Origin England
  • History First appeared from a natural mutation in Cornwall in 1950.
  • Size Small–Medium
  • Life Span 14-20 years
  • Characteristics Very short curly coat, large ears, slender athletic body.
  • Temperament Energetic, mischievous, affectionate, loves to be the center of attention.
  • Care tips Keep warm, gentle baths, interactive play.

Fun Fact!

Did you know? They have only the undercoat layer of fur, making their coat feel like velvet.

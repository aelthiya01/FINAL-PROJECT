Information

  • Place Origin Iran / Europe
  • History Selectively bred to produce tri-colored longhaired coats, especially popular among show breeders.
  • Size Medium–Large
  • Life Span 16-20 years
  • Characteristics Patches of black, orange, and white in long silky fur; round face with expressive eyes.
  • Temperament Gentle but with a bit more personality due to the tortie genetics—sweet yet independent.
  • Care tips Brush daily, keep coat clean, provide a calm environment.

Fun Fact!

Did you know? 99% are female because the three-color pattern requires XX chromosomes.

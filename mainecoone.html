Information

  • Place Origin United States (Maine)
  • History Natural breed; stories suggest Viking cats may be their ancestors.
  • Size Large
  • Life Span 9-15 years
  • Characteristics Long bushy tail, tufted ears, thick waterproof fur, strong build.
  • Temperament Friendly, confident, playful, and social; often called “gentle giants.”
  • Care tips Large scratching posts, regular brushing, interactive toys, plenty of space.

Fun Fact!

Did you know? Their fur is water-resistant, ideal for snowy climates.
